About Lung Cancer

Risk Factors

  • Smoking (primary risk factor)
  • Secondhand smoke exposure
  • Radon gas exposure
  • Asbestos exposure
  • Family history of lung cancer

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent cough
  • Coughing up blood
  • Chest pain
  • Hoarseness
  • Unexplained weight loss

Prevention Tips

  • Quit smoking immediately
  • Avoid secondhand smoke
  • Test home for radon
  • Avoid carcinogens at work
  • Eat a healthy diet
