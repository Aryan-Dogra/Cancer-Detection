About Skin Cancer

Risk Factors

  • Excessive UV exposure
  • Fair skin, light hair
  • History of sunburns
  • Many moles or atypical moles
  • Family history

Warning Signs (ABCDE)

  • Asymmetry in shape
  • Border irregularity
  • Color variation
  • Diameter larger than 6mm
  • Evolving size or appearance

Prevention Tips

  • Use sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • Wear protective clothing
  • Avoid peak sun hours
  • Regular skin self-exams
  • Annual dermatologist visits
