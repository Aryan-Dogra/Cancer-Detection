About Kidney Cancer

Risk Factors

  • Smoking
  • Obesity
  • High blood pressure
  • Family history
  • Long-term dialysis

Common Symptoms

  • Blood in urine
  • Lower back pain (one side)
  • Loss of appetite
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent fever

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Control blood pressure
  • Quit smoking
  • Stay hydrated
  • Regular exercise
